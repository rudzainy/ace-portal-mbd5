Participation & winning criteria

What to Build

Build or significantly update a software application using Amazon Comprehend Medical to address key challenges for health organizations to improve care quality and health equity.

Additional AWS services may be used including other AI/ML services like Amazon SageMaker.

What to Submit

Provide access to the code repository of your project by making it public or by sharing access with testing@devpost.com and awshealthaihackathon@amazon.com.

Include a video (about 3 minutes) that explains and demonstrates your project in action. Videos must be uploaded to YouTube, Vimeo, or Facebook Video and made public.

Your complete submission form on Devpost before the final deadline.
